Ab Crunch Machine / Plank

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Builds core stability and supports posture.

Instructions

  1. For machine: adjust pad and perform small crunches focusing on the core.
  2. For plank: hold a straight-body plank on forearms for 20–30s.
  3. Breathe steadily and keep core engaged.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid straining the neck during crunches.
  • Keep a neutral spine during planks.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain.

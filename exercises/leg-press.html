Seated Leg Press

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens quads, glutes, and hamstrings. Builds lower-body foundation safely.

Instructions

  1. Sit with back firmly against the pad and feet about shoulder-width on the platform.
  2. Press through your heels to extend your legs; avoid locking the knees at the top.
  3. Lower in a controlled manner until knees are at roughly 90 degrees, then press back up.

Safety Tips

  • Adjust seat so knees track in line with toes.
  • Start with light weight and focus on control.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain in knees or back.

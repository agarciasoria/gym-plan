Lat Pulldown

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens upper back and helps posture.

Instructions

  1. Adjust thigh pads to hold you steady.
  2. Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder-width and pull to upper chest.
  3. Control the return to the starting position.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid leaning back excessively.
  • Move with control and avoid momentum.
  • Keep shoulders down and back.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift

Free Weights • Posterior Chain

Home Exercises Progress Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens hamstrings, glutes, and lower back while teaching proper hip-hinge mechanics. Essential for posture, injury prevention, and long-term strength progression.

Instructions

  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with arms extended in front of thighs.
  2. Stand tall with feet hip-width apart and knees slightly bent.
  3. Push hips back while keeping your back flat and chest open.
  4. Lower the dumbbells along your legs toward mid-shin level.
  5. Feel the stretch in hamstrings—do not force depth.
  6. Drive hips forward to return to standing.

Safety Tips

  • Keep spine neutral—no rounding or arching.
  • Movement comes from hips, not the lower back.
  • Do not let dumbbells drift away from your legs.
  • Use lighter weights until technique is consistent.

Common Mistakes

  • Bending knees too much (turns it into a squat).
  • Rounding the lower back.
  • Lowering weights too far and losing control.

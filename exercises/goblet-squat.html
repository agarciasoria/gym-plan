Goblet Squat

Free-weight Lower Body Exercise

Home Exercises Progress Tracker

Purpose

Develops full lower-body strength while reinforcing proper squat mechanics. Excellent transition exercise from machines to free weights.

Instructions

  1. Hold a dumbbell or kettlebell close to your chest with both hands.
  2. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly turned out.
  3. Brace your core and keep your chest upright.
  4. Lower into a squat by pushing hips back and bending knees.
  5. Descend until thighs are at least parallel to the floor.
  6. Drive through your heels to return to standing.

Safety Tips

  • Keep the weight close to your body at all times.
  • Avoid rounding your lower back.
  • Do not let knees collapse inward.
  • Start light and increase weight gradually.

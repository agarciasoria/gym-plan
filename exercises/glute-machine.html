Glute Machine / Kickback

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Targets glutes and improves hip stability.

Instructions

  1. Position pad on the back of the lower thigh (or use the machine footplate).
  2. Push back/down through the heel, squeeze glute at the top.
  3. Return with control.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid arching lower back during movement.
  • Use light to moderate resistance.
  • Control the movement without momentum.

Seated Bicep Curl (Machine)

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Isolates biceps in a controlled, seated position.

Instructions

  1. Adjust seat and pad so elbows align with pivot.
  2. Curl slowly, squeeze at the top, lower with control.
  3. Avoid swinging shoulders.

Safety Tips

  • Keep elbows stationary and close to your body.
  • Use weight you can control for full reps.
  • Stop if you feel elbow pain.

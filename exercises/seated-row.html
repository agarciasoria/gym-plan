Seated Row

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Works mid-back, improves scapular control and posture.

Instructions

  1. Sit tall and keep a neutral spine.
  2. Pull handle toward your abdomen while squeezing shoulder blades.
  3. Return slowly and maintain control.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid rounding your back.
  • Use controlled tempo to prevent jerking.
  • Start light to feel the movement.

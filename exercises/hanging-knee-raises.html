Hanging Knee Raises

Bodyweight • Core & Pull-Up Preparation

Home Exercises Progress Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens the abdominal muscles, grip, and hip flexors while teaching controlled hanging mechanics. A key stepping stone toward pull-ups and hanging leg raises.

Instructions

  1. Hang from a pull-up bar with arms fully extended.
  2. Engage core by slightly tucking pelvis.
  3. Raise knees toward chest in a slow, controlled motion.
  4. Pause briefly at the top.
  5. Lower legs slowly without swinging.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid swinging or using momentum.
  • Keep shoulders active (slight shoulder engagement).
  • If grip is limiting, use straps temporarily.

Progressions

  • Beginner: Captain’s chair knee raises.
  • Standard: Hanging knee raises.
  • Advanced: Hanging leg raises.

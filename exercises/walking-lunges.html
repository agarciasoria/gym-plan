Walking Lunges

Bodyweight / Dumbbells • Unilateral Strength

Home Exercises Progress Tracker

Purpose

Develops leg strength, balance, coordination, and hip stability. Excellent for correcting left–right imbalances and building functional lower-body strength.

Instructions

  1. Stand tall with feet together.
  2. Step forward with one leg into a long stride.
  3. Lower back knee toward the floor under control.
  4. Front knee should track over toes, not cave inward.
  5. Push through the front heel to step forward with the opposite leg.
  6. Continue alternating steps.

Safety Tips

  • Keep torso upright—avoid leaning forward.
  • Control each step; do not rush.
  • Start bodyweight only before adding dumbbells.

Progressions

  • Beginner: Short steps, bodyweight.
  • Intermediate: Full stride walking lunges.
  • Advanced: Hold dumbbells at sides.

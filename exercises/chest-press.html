Seated Chest Press

Exercise Guide

Home Exercises Progress Tracker

Purpose

Builds chest, shoulders, and triceps; improves pushing strength.

Instructions

  1. Adjust seat so handles align with mid-chest.
  2. Grip handles, keep shoulders down and back, press forward until arms are nearly straight.
  3. Return slowly to start position with control.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid shrugging shoulders up during press.
  • Use a weight that allows controlled motion.
  • Keep core engaged.

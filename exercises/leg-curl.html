Leg Curl (Seated)

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Targets hamstrings and helps balance leg strength.

Instructions

  1. Adjust the pad so it rests just above the heels/ankles.
  2. Curl your heels back toward your glutes in a controlled motion.
  3. Squeeze briefly at the top, then lower slowly.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid jerky movements.
  • Do not lift hips off the seat.
  • Start light to learn movement pattern.

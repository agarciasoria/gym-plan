Seated Abductor

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens hip abductors for pelvic stability and knee support.

Instructions

  1. Sit upright with knees together against the inner pads.
  2. Push outward against the pads, then return slowly.
  3. Keep torso still and breathe steadily.

Safety Tips

  • Avoid leaning back or forward during the movement.
  • Start with light resistance.
  • Maintain a steady tempo.

Shoulder Press (Seated)

Home | Exercises | Tracker

Purpose

Strengthens deltoids and triceps; supports good posture.

Instructions

  1. Adjust seat so handles are near shoulder height.
  2. Press upward without locking elbows, then lower slowly.
  3. Maintain a neutral spine and avoid overarching.

Safety Tips

  • Use moderate weight to avoid shoulder strain.
  • Stop if you experience sharp shoulder pain.
  • Keep movements controlled.
